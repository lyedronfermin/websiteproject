Burnham Park

Burnham Park in Baguio City, Philippines, is a 32.84-hectare urban park named after Daniel Hudson Burnham, an American architect. It features a man-made lake for boating, gardens, picnic areas, and playgrounds. The park hosts events like concerts and festivals and is popular for jogging and cycling. It's a vibrant hub in the heart of the city.
